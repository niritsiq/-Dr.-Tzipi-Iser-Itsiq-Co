Accessibility Statement

Introduction:

Dr. Tzipi Iser Itsiq & Co. Law Firm considers it of utmost importance to make its website accessible to people with disabilities. We invest significant efforts to enable convenient and accessible browsing for the entire population, in accordance with the Equal Rights for Persons with Disabilities Law and its regulations.

The website was built in accordance with the accessibility guidelines appearing in W3C's Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 2.1, Level AA.

Accessibility Menu

The site features an advanced accessibility menu that allows:

  • Contrast adjustment (High, Low, Grayscale)
  • Font resizing (Increase/Decrease)
  • Readable font and Dyslexia-friendly font
  • Link highlighting
  • Cursor enlargement and color change
  • Hiding images to reduce visual load
  • Page Reading (Text-to-Speech)
  • Enhanced Keyboard Navigation

Browser Compatibility

The site has been tested and supports common browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Safari
  • Opera

Physical Accessibility Arrangements

Our office is located at Icon Tower, 13 Menachem Begin Rd, Ramat Gan.

  • Disabled parking spaces are available in the building's parking lot.
  • There is an accessible route from the parking lot to the elevators and the office.
  • Disabled restrooms are available in the building.
  • Service animals are welcome in the office.
